Welcome to the greatest Trivia Game of all time

How to play:
Make an account or log into a pre-existing account.

Click the 'plus' icon in the nav bar to go to our game setup page.

It is within this page you'll decide these main game factors:
Category (general knowledge, books, film, music, musical/theatre, or all categories),
Difficulty (easy, medium, hard or mixed),
Type of question (true/false, multiple choice or both),
Number of players (solo players won't have any impact on overall games won or lost for that user's stats),
and lastly, you'll get to pick up to 3 other users to play in your game with you.

The game will alternate which players answer which questions, where at the end of the gme the scores are tallied up.

The game will calculate each player's statistics to determine where their weaknesses and strengths are, and uploading
their overall stats through previous games online.
